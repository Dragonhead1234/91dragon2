The game currently has six game modes: Hill Control, Domination, Team Deathmatch (TDM), Free For All (FFA), Artifact (Arti), Gun game, and Capture the Flag (CTF). A match in Energy Assault lasts for 10 minutes (it used to be 15 minutes during early versions) and the team with the highest score wins. Game time can also be changed on private servers with the Private Server Controls gamepass (you can only change the settings on your own private server), which costs 899 Robux.

Team deathmatch

One point is awarded for each kill. The team with the most points after 10 minutes wins.

Domination

There are 3 objectives (A, B, C) on each map. Each objective gives the team controlling them 1 point every 3 seconds. You get 100 EXP for neutralizing an objective that the enemy has captured and 200 more for capturing. After 10 minutes, the team with the most points wins.

Hill control

1 point is awarded to the team controlling the hill every 1 second. You have to maintain your position on the hill to control it. After 10 minutes, the team with the most points wins.

Artifact

1 point is awarded to your team every 1 second that you or a teammate has the artifact (which is an object with an "ARTIFACT" marker). After 10 minutes, the team with the most points wins.
